About

I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in EECS at MIT exploring the theoretical limits of equivariant neural networks. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tess Smidt in the Atomic Architects group.

I previously completed my B.S. in Physics and Math at MIT.

My work has been supported by the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship and the MIT Schwarzman College of Computing Fellowship.
